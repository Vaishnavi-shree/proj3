👯‍♀️ Meet My Two Amazing Friends

💫 Nandini - The Silent Pataka💥

Don’t go on her calm face – she’s the mastermind behind 99% of the silent chaos. 😈 Whether it’s skipping lectures or dropping sarcastic bombs, Ancy does it with elegance. Also, don’t challenge her in *"Who can stay quiet longer"* – you’ll lose.

When I first met Nandini, I thought she came with a built-in silence feature. 😅 Quiet, calm, and low-key mysterious.

But fast forward to now – we’re watching movies till 3 AM, cracking jokes that only sleep-deprived brains understand, and laughing like tomorrow’s a holiday (even when it’s not). 😆🎬

Late-night memories, unstoppable laughter 🌙💛

🎭 Ancy - The Loud Drama Queen 🎤

If you need a laugh, just sit next to Ancy. 😂 She’s the queen of dramatic expressions and loud outbursts. Her life is a never-ending comedy show, and we’re all just her audience. But beware – she might drag you into her drama too!

If you hear uncontrollable laughter from nowhere – that’s probably Ancy. 😄 Her energy is like a powerbank — she keeps us charged! Warning: Sitting next to her in a serious class is a guaranteed punishment. 😜

😎 Together = Chaos Unleashed 🚀

One brings the quiet drama, the other the loud comedy – and I’m just stuck between these two legends. 😂
Blessed to survive with them. Not sure how long my sanity will last though...
